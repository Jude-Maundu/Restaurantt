ABOUT US

We Believe Life Is All About Living.
Eat, Drink, Don’t Think

Leafy green surroundings, a cool breeze, great food and a relaxed ambience makes About Thyme stand out as one of the rare restaurants that allows you to immerse yourself in the best experience of al fresco dining within the city in a lush, sunken, multi-level garden setting with secret nooks and hidden crannies. In the evenings, fairy lights, candles and charcoal braziers set the tone for a warm and romantic atmosphere with a cozy indoor dining option complete with a wood-burning fireplace for the colder months.

A restaurant that has retained its tranquil setting in the heart of Westlands since 2005, About Thyme has become known for quality and consistency, offering imaginative and creative dishes inspired from around the world. Our menu will take you on an exciting culinary journey through Africa, Asia and Europe with dishes that are guaranteed to excite everyone. Made using locally sourced and sustainable ingredients, several of our dishes have become firm favorites among our customers over the years, with popular dishes like our ‘Tuna Tartar’, ‘Baked Camembert’, ‘Greek Moussaka’, ‘Pumpkin and Amaretti Tortellini’, ‘Chicken Pot Pie’ and ‘Kamba wa Kupaka’ being must tries. Desserts here are a special treat with our ‘Irresistible Sticky Date Pudding’ and ‘Peanut Butter Ice-Cream Pie' having a loyal following of their own. For the more adventurous, we have a specials menu that changes seasonally to ensure we keep you (and ourselves!) on your toes! A comprehensive wine list and extensive cocktail menu rounds off the experience.

Just like our namesake Thyme, our food is fresh and flavorful. We are open from Tuesday to Sunday serving lunch, dinner and weekend brunch and ready to welcome you to a unique, laid back and unpretentious dining experience.

We believe life is all about living, hence our motto, EAT, DRINK, DON’T THINK at About Thyme.
